Partner Center Emulation

This lab is a simulated walkthrough of creating an Azure Managed Application offer in the Partner Center portal.

The lab uses a simulation instead of asking you to use the actual Partner Center site so you don't have to join the Microsoft Partner Network, create an MPN ID, and Partner Center account, all of which can take some time.

If you already have access to the Partner Center portal, feel free to open it in another browser window and follow along with the lab.
